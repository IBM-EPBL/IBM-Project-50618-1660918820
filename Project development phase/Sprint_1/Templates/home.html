About

* “PLASMA DONOR APPLICATION” for getting plasma to the patient. In this application, the patient can find the donor through internet.

*If a person has diagnosed with a novel diseases (“CORONA VIRUS or COVID-19”).The Patient will register their details through this internet application.

*It helps the patient easy to apply via phone (a stress free application). The Registration process is very simple method such as user name, date of birth, phone number, age, blood group and nationality. This step involved for the patient. If a person should donate their blood (PLASMA), the person could register through this application.

*When the patient blood group and donor blood group is same, the patient can send request to the donor. If the donor accept the request they can contact each other and share their details, suppose the donor will not accept the request the patient can search for another donor
